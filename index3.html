A B O U T

One of the most humane organizations in Nigeria, founded shortly after the Civial war, the Animal Shelter has seen vast changes in the society, the environment, and the roles of animals in our lives. It has helped make laws and set standards that have fundamentally shaped our sense of kindness and care for animals and for one another. Today, the Animal Shelter continues to rescue, shelter, protect, heal, and advocate for more animals than any other Nigerian humane organization, giving hands-in care to many thousands of animals each year. We also provide emergency assistance and strategic-planning help for animal-protection groups across the country and around the globe. Through our legislative work, humane-education efforts and community-based assistance initiatives, we help create lasting change for animals and people.
